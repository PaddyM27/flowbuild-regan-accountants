About Us

Trusted advisors.
Clear answers.

Regan & Company has been helping Irish businesses and individuals navigate their financial lives for over 25 years. With offices in Dublin and Carrickmacross, Co. Monaghan, we combine the reach of a city practice with the personal service of a local firm.

Our team of qualified Chartered Accountants takes time to understand your situation before making recommendations — because good advice starts with listening. Whether you're a sole trader just starting out or an established SME, you'll always deal directly with a senior accountant.

"We believe every client deserves straightforward advice, delivered with care — not jargon, not delay."
